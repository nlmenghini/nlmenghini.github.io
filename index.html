About me

I am a passionate designer who enjoys exploring all creative graphic solutions for any design issue. I lean towards a clean, minimal aesthetic. I have a soft spot for grid based sensibilities and a passion for typography. I also nerd out on icon systems and create them in my free time.


I spent my formative years living and traveling abroad; leading to adventures rooted in cultural exploration and experiencing different environments. It gives me a unique cultural perspective that I try to impart onto my design philosophy.
