    URBANBOUND

    Standardized Reporting

    The project aimed at completely rebuilding our employer reporting system. The current system made it hard to make copies of reports, save filter sets, and schedule exports.

    We already knew the core set of reports that we would offer. I did some initial design exploration to help narrow down the possible columns and filters for each report. The card based reports page provides an easy, scannable interface with a limit set of reports. I designed the reports around a notion of versioning to kill the clutter of endless copies. An employer user can save any number of data sets and filters as a version. Once saved an employer can schedule any number of exports for that version.

    Main reports page. A card view provides an easy, scannable interface.

    Relocation Plan

    A relocation is long and complicated process. At the center is a policy that drives what an employee can do, how we can help them, and how much an employer wants to spend.

    The challenge with the plan was that each user has different needs from the same set of information. The employee app I focused on managing the budget and reviewing/booking services. The consultant app I focused on the ability to provide guidance. The employer app I focused on highlighting the spend of the move.

    The employee plan page; they can read notes from their consultant, review suppliers, and upload expenses.

    Order and Lead Management System

    The Order and Leads Management System (OLMS) is a refresh of UrbanBound's existing supplier application. The focus was to create a new order process that would allow suppliers to create many cost estimates for a single order.

    During the design process I focused on a modular system that would allow us to fine tune the order form and the employee display. The form and the display can handle any permutation estimate options, default form line items, or the addition of custom line items.

    URBANBOUND

    Cost Calculator

    Calculating costs is a huge part of any relocation. UrbanBound puts a high priority on being transparent with these costs. An in app cost calculator is something we have talked about for some time. This was part a team project for a hackathon.

    While my teammates figured out the proper math and back end coding, I prototyped the calculator. My goal was to keep with the spirit of transparency. The more information you provide the more accurate your estimate becomes. Even though the form narrows down the total you never lose sight of all potential costs.

    A cost breakdown form for relocations that becomes more accurate as you provide more information.
